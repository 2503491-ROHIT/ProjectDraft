Role Responsibilities & UI Flow
ADMIN
Responsibilities
Manage employee accounts.
Assign and update user roles.
Activate/Deactivate users.
Monitor customers, loans, repayments, and collections.
Assign or reassign delinquent cases to Collection Agents.
View reports and audit logs.
Dashboard
Show directly:
Pending Collection Cases to Assign
Total Customers
Total Loan Applications
Approved Loans
Active Loans
Overdue Loans
Recovery Statistics
CUSTOMER
Responsibilities
Register and login.
Complete KYC.
Apply for a loan.
Track application status.
View credit score.
View repayment schedule.
Pay EMIs.
View payment history.
Dashboard
Show directly:
KYC Status
Active Loan
Current Application Status
Next EMI
Outstanding Balance
Recent Payments
LOAN OFFICER
Responsibilities
Review newly submitted KYC requests.
Verify KYC documents.
Review newly submitted loan applications.
Perform eligibility assessment.
Approve initial verification.
Forward eligible applications to the Credit Analyst.
Reject incomplete applications.
After Underwriter approval, execute loan disbursement through the Disbursement Service.
Dashboard
Show directly:
Pending KYC Verification
New Loan Applications
Applications Waiting for Eligibility Check
Applications Ready to Forward to Credit Analyst
Loans Approved by Underwriter Waiting for Disbursement
Recently Disbursed Loans
CREDIT ANALYST
Responsibilities
Review applications forwarded by the Loan Officer.
Fetch bureau score.
Calculate internal score.
Evaluate customer risk.
Assign risk grade.
Add evaluation remarks.
Forward evaluated applications to the Underwriter.
Dashboard
Show directly:
Applications Waiting for Credit Evaluation
High Risk Applications
Medium Risk Applications
Low Risk Applications
Recently Evaluated Applications
UNDERWRITER
Responsibilities
Review applications evaluated by the Credit Analyst.
Perform final loan assessment.
Approve or reject loans.
Set approved amount.
Set interest rate.
Set tenure.
Forward approved loans to the Loan Officer for disbursement.
Dashboard
Show directly:
Applications Awaiting Final Approval
Approved Loans
Rejected Loans
Recently Processed Applications
COLLECTION AGENT
Responsibilities
View assigned delinquent accounts.
Contact customers.
Record recovery actions.
Update recovery status.
Record payment commitments.
Close recovered cases.
Recommend escalation or write-off.
Dashboard
Show directly:
Newly Assigned Recovery Cases
Today's Follow-ups
Customers to Contact Today
Pending Recovery Actions
Recovered Cases
Overdue Accounts
Complete Workflow
Customer registers and completes KYC.
KYC request appears automatically in the Loan Officer Dashboard.
Loan Officer verifies KYC.
Customer submits a loan application.
The loan application appears automatically in the Loan Officer Dashboard.
Loan Officer reviews the application and performs the eligibility assessment.
Eligible applications are forwarded to the Credit Analyst.
The application appears automatically in the Credit Analyst Dashboard.
Credit Analyst evaluates the application and forwards it to the Underwriter.
The application appears automatically in the Underwriter Dashboard.
Underwriter approves or rejects the application.
Approved loans appear automatically in the Loan Officer Dashboard for disbursement.
Loan Officer initiates disbursement through the Disbursement Service, which generates the repayment schedule.
Customer pays EMIs.
If an EMI becomes overdue, the Collection Service creates a delinquency case.
The case appears in the Admin Dashboard for assignment.
Admin assigns the case to a Collection Agent.
The assigned case appears automatically in the Collection Agent Dashboard for recovery.

roles  and resposnsibilitses:

Role
Responsibilities
ADMIN
Manage users, assign roles, activate/deactivate accounts, assign/reassign collection cases, monitor the system, view reports and audit logs.
CUSTOMER
Register, login, complete KYC, update profile, apply for loans, track application status, view credit score, view EMI schedule, make EMI payments, view repayment history.
LOAN_OFFICER
Verify KYC and documents, validate eligibility, review applications, request additional documents, execute loan disbursement by invoking the Disbursement Service after final approval, generate the EMI schedule, and update the loan status to DISBURSED.
CREDIT_ANALYST
Fetch bureau score, calculate internal credit score, assess repayment capacity, assign risk grade, add remarks, and forward applications to the Underwriter.
UNDERWRITER
Review the complete application, make the final approval or rejection, determine the sanctioned amount, interest rate, and tenure, and forward approved loans for disbursement.
COLLECTION_AGENT
Handle assigned delinquent accounts, contact customers, record recovery actions, update recovery status, close recovered cases, and recommend escalation or write-off.
